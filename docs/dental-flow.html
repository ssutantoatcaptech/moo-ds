What's Covered

Your plan covers different types of dental care at different levels.

Preventive Procedures
Fully paid by your dental insurance. No deductible. Payments for these services do not count toward your annual maximum.
Includes: Dental Exams, X-Rays, Fluoride Treatments, Sealants, Routine Cleanings, Brush Biopsy
Basic Procedures
Partially covered after deductible. Plan pays 80%.
Includes: Fillings, Extractions, Root Canals, Periodontal Treatment
Major Procedures
Partially covered after deductible. Plan pays 50%.
Includes: Crowns, Bridges, Dentures, Implants

What is an ADA Code?

ADA codes are standard procedure codes dentists use to show what treatment was done. They help your dental plan know exactly what was performed so your insurance benefits can be applied correctly. Search for specific procedures by name or ADA code to learn more about the care, category, and how it is covered.
